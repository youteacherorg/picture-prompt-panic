## How to download from Unsplash (free, no account needed)
1. Click the search link below
2. Pick any photo you like from the results
3. Click the photo → click the Download button (↓ free download)
4. Rename the file to the exact filename shown below
5. Put it in your `images/` folder

## Tips
- **Landscape orientation works best** (wider than tall). Avoid portrait/vertical shots.
- **Images ~1–3 MB** are fine. You don't need huge files.
- You can use **any image** — Google Images, your own photos, Pexels, etc. Just rename the file correctly.
- Want to **replace a topic**? Edit `index.html` and change the `topic`, `detail`, and `img` fields in the PAIRS array.

---

## Opening the Game
- Double-click `index.html` — it opens in Chrome
- Press **SPACEBAR** to advance through stages (easier than clicking with a full class watching)
- Stages: Image appears → SPACEBAR → 30s prep timer → SPACEBAR → 2min speaking timer
- All 20 pairs play in shuffled order with no repeats, then reshuffle automatically
